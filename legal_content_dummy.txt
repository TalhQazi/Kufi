FAQs

How do I book an experience?
Browse our available experiences, select the one you want, choose your preferred date and time, and complete the checkout process. You will receive a confirmation email once your booking is confirmed.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?
Yes, modifications and cancellations are possible depending on the specific experience and how close it is to the start date. Please check the cancellation policy listed on each experience page or contact us for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), PayPal, Google Pay, and other secure digital payment methods. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

How do I contact my service provider?
Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive contact information for your service provider in your confirmation email. You can reach out to them directly for specific questions about your experience.

What if I have a problem during my experience?
If you encounter any issues during your experience, please contact the service provider first. If the issue is not resolved, reach out to our support team through the Support section and we will assist you.

Do you offer refunds?
Refund policies vary by experience and are determined by individual service providers. Please review the specific cancellation and refund policy listed on each experience page before booking.

How do I create an account?
Click on the Login/Signup button at the top of our website and follow the registration process. Creating an account allows you to save favorites, track bookings, and receive exclusive offers.

Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and payment information. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.

Common Issues

Booking Problems
If you're having trouble completing a booking, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. Ensure your payment information is correct and up to date.

Account Access
Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team for assistance.

Experience Questions
For specific questions about an experience, such as meeting points, what to bring, or accessibility options, please contact the service provider directly using the contact information in your booking confirmation.

Refunds and Cancellations
To request a cancellation or refund, log into your account and navigate to your bookings. Follow the cancellation process outlined there. For issues with refunds, please contact our support team with your booking reference number.

Feedback
We value your feedback! If you have suggestions for improving our services or want to share your experience, please email us at feedback@kufitravel.com. Your input helps us serve you better.

Emergency Assistance
For urgent matters during your travel, please contact the local emergency services first. For trip-related emergencies, use the emergency contact number provided in your booking confirmation.
